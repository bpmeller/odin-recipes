Ingredients

  • 1 turkey, 5-15 lb. (thawed)
  • 1-2 cups whipped butter (room temperature)
  • 2 cup chicken broth
  • 1-4 tbsp garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Liberally coat the turkey with the whipped butter mixture. Ensure the entire surface is coated.
  2. Place turkey in a foil baking pan with mixed vegetables. If available, use a rack to elevate the turkey from the bottom of the pan.
  3. Add chicken broth until it coats the bottom of the pan.
  4. Ignite the charcoal grill; arrange the coals for indirect cooking. Try to maintain a temperature of 300-400°F.
  5. Place the turkey in pan on the grill and cook indirectly for 1 hour.
  6. Remove when the turkey reaches an internal temperature of 160°F in the breast.
